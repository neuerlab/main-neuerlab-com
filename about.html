She cleared and prepared the space verbally asking only for light and reiterated my intensions. The first couple energies that came through I could not understand how they were linked to me. When Nicola first encountered my mom, Nicola was hesitant, and for understandable reasons – I immediately said, "that's my mom." Nicola cleared the room again: reinforcing only light beings were invited and asking for my mom (by name) to come forth. Nicola described a space that matched and reinforced a dream I had with my mom days prior, which sent me to tears while I slept. One of the things my mom said was, "I left you something" showing Nicola where to find "writing" or a "letter." Six days later I walked into my mom's room, where she had died and my sister was holding up the addressed writing to me. I asked my sister where she had found the paperwork. Just as my mom instructed Nicola, the papers were found in a cupboard, in a skinny drawer, under a mirror. When I told my sister of meeting with a medium who directed me to that exact drawer under the mirror, my sister turned white. I am grateful for the conversation I had with my mom, as it allowed some grounding after her passing, even if some parts of the session didn't make sense – I am aware that setting an intention of being open to what comes through, may yield information I do not yet understand."
